SeelViz: Data Visualization of Clarity Brain Images

SeelViz (short for Clarity Visualization) is a collection of tools used to analyze and visualize CLARITY-treated brains. This website is maintained to provide deliverables of our weekly progress as well as extensive documentation regarding our projects.
